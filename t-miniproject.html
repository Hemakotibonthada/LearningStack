Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the united Stated?

Tacos were first introduced to the United States in 1905 by Mexican migrant workers who brought the food with them to work on railroads and other jobs: Early days At first, tacos were considered lower-class street food. Hard-shell tacos The earliest references to hard-shell tacos are from the early 1890s. Glen Bell, the founder of Taco Bell, claimed to have invented the hard-shell taco in 1952. Ameri-Mex foods By 1920, Mexican immigrants' food began to fuse with American ingredients, creating Ameri-Mex foods. Fast food chains In the mid-1950s, fast food chains began to market hard-shell tacos to Americans. Taco Bell played a significant role in popularizing the food. Authentic Mexican restaurants Authentic Mexican restaurants are now bringing the real taste of Mexico back to the menu. Some other regional Mexican tacos that have become popular in the U.S. include: Tacos al pastor Invented by second-generation Lebanese Mexicans in the 1960s, this taco features spit-grilled boneless pork, pineapple, and a tortilla.
